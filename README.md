# Guns Wihtout Roses Expansions

This mod expands the Guns Without Roses mod with powerful new weapons, unique ammunition types, and cross-mod integrations.

## Vanilla GunsWithoutRoses

**Netherite Tier Weapons**:  
* Netherite Shotgun
* Netherite Sniper
* Netherite Gatling
* Netherite Monster Shotgun

**Special Ammunition**:
* Slime Bullet - Bounces between multiple targets
* Netherite Bullet - Reusable with recovery chance
* Diamond Bullet - Splits into piercing fragments
* Golden Bullet - May shatter into gold nuggets or rarely a golden apple

## Ice and Fire Integration

**Dragonsteel Weapons**:  
* Fire Dragonsteel (Shotgun/Sniper/Gatling) - Ignites targets, bonus damage against Ice Dragons
* Ice Dragonsteel (Shotgun/Sniper/Gatling) - Freezes targets, bonus damage against Fire Dragons
* Lightning Dragonsteel (Shotgun/Sniper/Gatling) - Strikes targets with lightning

**Special Ammunition**:
* Dragonsteel Elemental Bullets - Fire/Ice/Lightning variants
* Silver Bullets - Extra damage against undead, can be converted to elemental bullets

## Cataclysm Integration

**Weapon Series**:  
* Cursium Sniper - Auto-tracking bullets that summon phantasmal halberds on headshots
* Ignitium Gatling - Life-stealing bullets that apply Blazing Brand
* Tidal Pistol - Applies Abyssal Curse, can create dimensional portals
* Lavapower Shotgun - Creates cross-shaped flame jets

**Special Ammunition**:
* Cursium Bullets - Track enemies automatically
* Ignitium Bullets - Apply Blazing Brand effect
* Lavapower Bullets - Create flame jets on impact
* Tidal Bullets - Apply stacking Abyssal Curse

## Bosses of Mass Destruction Integration

**Weapon Series**:
* Hellforge Revolver - Marks targets and throws coins that bullets can ricochet between
* Obsidian Launcher - Fires Obsidian Cores that cast random spells (Fire/Frost/Holy)
* Skullcrusher Pulverizer - Crushes skulls into bone fragments, gains speed and damage over time
* Void Spike - Rapid-fire biological weapon that launches healing spores

**Special Mechanics**:
* Coin System - Earn coins by shooting, throw them to create ricochet points for tracking bullets
* Spell Casting - Obsidian Cores cast different magical effects
* Bone Fragments - Skull ammunition shatters into multiple piercing projectiles
* Healing Spores - Bud bullets explode into spores that heal the shooter when hitting enemies

## Universal Features

* Full configuration options for damage, fire delay, and other weapon parameters
* Achievement system with unique challenges for each weapon type
* Custom sounds and visual effects for all weapons
* Meat Hook system for Super Shotgun (DOOM-inspired)

## Requirements

* Minecraft Forge 1.16.5
* Guns Without Roses ≥2.10
* Ice and Fire ≥2.1.13 (Optional)
* Cataclysm ≥2.3.1 (Optional)
* Bosses of Mass Destruction ≥1.1.1 (Optional)
